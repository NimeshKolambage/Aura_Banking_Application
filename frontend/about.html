Our Story

Founded in 2018, Aura Bank was born from a simple yet powerful idea: banking should be effortless, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

What started as a vision to challenge traditional banking has evolved into a thriving community of over 2 million customers who trust us with their financial futures. Our founders, a team of fintech innovators and banking veterans, recognized that the financial industry needed a radical transformation.

Today, we combine cutting-edge technology with a human-centered approach, offering zero-fee accounts, intelligent savings tools, and personalized financial guidance. We're not just a bank—we're your financial partner, committed to helping you achieve more with your money.
